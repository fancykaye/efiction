- - - - - - READ ME - - - - - -

It is of great importance that you add the following line

<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0"/>

within the <head>...</head> section of the header.php (main directory of your efiction folder). Should be right after line 218.
Without this change the layout will not response to mobile devices.

That's it.

Enjoy! :)
